❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do employees log their location?

Two options: via daily email (one-click "Home" or "Office" buttons) or through our mobile app available on iOS and Android. Both take just seconds.

Where is my data stored?

We value your privacy and compliance. unique. All data is securely stored on servers located within the European Union (Germany), ensuring full GDPR compliance.

Which countries are supported?

Currently France (34 days max), Belgium (24 days max), and Germany (≤183 days). We're adding more European countries based on customer demand.

What happens during an audit?

You can export a detailed compliance report showing every employee's remote work days for any date range. Reports include timestamps and employee confirmations.

Is our data secure and GDPR compliant?

Yes. We're GDPR compliant, data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we use SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure. We only store employee names, emails, and work location declarations.

Can I try it before committing?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

What if an employee forgets to declare?

The HR dashboard shows who hasn't declared yet. You can send manual reminders or enable automatic follow-up emails at custom times.
